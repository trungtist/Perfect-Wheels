Standard

A standard car is standard-sized, which means it is bigger than a compact car but not quite as big as a full-size sedan. It is easy to seat five comfortably in a standard sedan. A standard car also has room to accommodate luggage and provides a pleasant drive, no matter the distance.

Minivan

A minivan is a vehicle built atop a platform of a small car with a low body, sliding or hinged rear doors, and 3 rows of seats that fit 8-9 passengers in total.
